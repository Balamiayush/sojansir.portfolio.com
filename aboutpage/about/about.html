Engineer, Innovator, and Leader Driving Nepal’s Transformation

I began my journey studying Mechanical Engineering at Kathmandu University, where I built a strong foundation in engineering and problem-solving. After graduation, I worked as a Plant Engineer in a cement factory, gaining hands-on industry experience.

Driven by a passion for energy and technology, I pursued my Master’s in Power Engineering at the Technical University of Munich (TUM)—a field that blends mechanical and electrical engineering. I specialized in aerodynamics and mathematical optimization of aero-structural wind turbines, focusing on renewable energy innovation.

Alongside academics, I worked as an Energy Consultant at the SEED Center (Sustainable Energy, Entrepreneurship and Development) at TUM, where I developed a biogas business case in Makonde, Zimbabwe. I also gained global consulting exposure at Boston Consulting Group (BCG), one of the world’s leading consulting firms.

Returning to Nepal, I helped establish and grow one of Nepal’s largest innovation centers at Advanced College of Engineering and Management, creating a hub for research, entrepreneurship, and technology. From this foundation, I co-founded and built several ventures, including Speak Up Nepal, Kathmandu Ko Katha, Digital Mantras, and Amplify Digital.

Always exploring...

Currently, I am the Founder of Engineers Without Borders Nepal and the Chief Business Officer of Ting Ting, one of the fastest-growing AI startups in Nepal, working to redefine communication technology and digital transformation in the region.
