TingTing

Ting Ting is Nepal’s pioneering AI-powered telephony and communication suite. Designed to empower businesses, institutions, and local governments, Ting Ting enables large-scale, cost-effective, and personalized communication through outbound AI voice calls, SMS, and other automated solutions.

Challenge

Business communication in Nepal is very fragmented. Every company wants to reach its customers, but there is no single and effective way to do it. Ting Ting started by focusing on outbound communication, because even here the problems are huge.

A human caller can make around 70 calls in one day. But a typical business has more than half a million customers. Reaching everyone manually is simply impossible. Businesses try to solve this with push notifications or SMS campaigns. But both have major limits.

Most Nepalese own a smartphone, but many do not know how to use apps properly. So push notifications often don’t reach or make sense to them. SMS campaigns are also not effective, because they feel impersonal, and businesses cannot really track or take data-driven decisions from them.

Solution

Ting Ting brings all communication under one roof, starting with outbound calls. Instead of relying only on SMS or app notifications, Ting Ting uses AI-powered voice calls that can reach thousands of people in just a few minutes. The system is simple, scalable, and designed for Nepal.

With Ting Ting, a business does not need hundreds of human callers. The platform can run large campaigns automatically and deliver messages in a personal voice. It also supports local languages, so people feel more connected and trust the message.

Technology

Ting Ting is powered by its own text-to-speech engine called Riri. This allows any written information to be converted into a natural voice, so messages can be delivered in a way that feels personal and easy to understand.

Behind the scenes, Ting Ting also has its own telephony orchestration layer. This is the system that manages and connects all the calls at scale. Because of this, Ting Ting can run up to one million outbound calls in a single day, something no human team could ever achieve.
